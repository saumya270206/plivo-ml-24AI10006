Project Summary

Small GPT trained under a 2,000,000-parameter / 2,000-step budget on a bilingual (English + Devanagari) corpus.

1. Architecture

ComponentChoiceWhy
TokenizerByte-level BPE, vocab_size=768Fixes the byte/character imbalance between English (1 byte/char) and Devanagari (3 bytes/char) under a fixed byte-level block_size; lossless by construction since it only merges byte spans.
Positional encodingRoPE (rotary), replacing learned pos_embZero learned parameters vs. ~20K for a learned absolute table; encodes relative offsets, which should generalize at least as well.
Layers / heads / dimn_layer=6, n_head=4, n_embd=160Depth increased from a 4-layer baseline, funded by weight tying + RoPE's freed parameters.
EmbeddingsTied (head.weight = tok_emb.weight)Saves vocab_size × n_embd params entirely (Press & Wolf, 2017); reinvested into depth.
InitGPT-2 style: std=0.02, residual projections scaled by 1/sqrt(2·n_layer)Prevents residual-stream variance from compounding across 6 stacked blocks.
MLPGELU, 4x expansionStandard; not changed from baseline.
OptimizerAdamW, weight decay on 2D+ params onlyDecaying biases/LayerNorm/embeddings fights normalization for no benefit.
LR scheduleLinear warmup (100 steps) → cosine decay to 10% peak LRAvoids early-step instability, then anneals smoothly.
Grad clippingMax norm 1.0Guards against spikes, especially important early in training.
Batch size64 (up from 8)Uncapped by the assignment — only steps and n_params are capped — so this is a free ~8x increase in total tokens seen over the same 2000 steps.
Params1,978,880 / 2,000,000 cap21,120 headroom remaining under the cap.

Why this combination, not something else

The three fixed budgets in this assignment are steps, parameters, and the corpus itself — so the highest-leverage changes are the ones that spend those budgets more efficiently rather than adding raw capacity. Batch size is the clearest example: it doesn't count against any cap, so raising it is close to a free win in both total tokens seen and gradient variance. RoPE is the second: it replaces a parameter-hungry, non-extrapolating positional scheme with a zero-parameter one, which either helps directly or at minimum frees capacity for the transformer body. Weight tying, GPT-2 init, and the AdamW/schedule/clipping combination were carried over from an earlier iteration that already validated at bpb 2.2492 before RoPE was introduced.

3. How to reproduce

cd starter
python train_bpe.py --data ../data/train_corpus.txt --vocab_size 768 --out bpe_merges.json
python train.py --data ../data/train_corpus.txt --steps 2000 --out ckpt.pt
python evaluate.py --checkpoint ckpt.pt --text_file ../data/dev_eval.txt
